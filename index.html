Easy to Use

Embex has been designed to keep role maintaining low, with quick setups and intuitive configuration.

Why Embex?

Embex is a bot designed to require no embed knowlage and can accessable used by anyone. Embex also has a variety of options to make you're embeds eye catching.

Coded in Discord.js v12

Discord.js v12 is a very powerful and fast api that allows quick interactions, consistency, and performance.
